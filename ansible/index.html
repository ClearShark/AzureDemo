Discover Stuttgart's Rich History

Stuttgart's history dates back over a thousand years, and its historic charm is still evident today. Visit the stunning Stuttgart Palace, a testament to the city's royal past, or explore the captivating Old Town with its cobblestone streets and picturesque squares. You can also delve into the fascinating history of the Mercedes-Benz and Porsche car museums, which showcase the city's contributions to the automotive industry.
